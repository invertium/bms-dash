Support for password-protected BMS Bluetooth modules. If your pack asks for
a password, you no longer have to open the Xiaoxiang app first to unlock
communication — enter the 6-character password when prompted and BMS Dash
remembers it for that pack, stored in the Android keystore.

Testers: this is new and unverified on real locked hardware. If your pack
unlocks but then shows no readings, please report it — that points at one
specific known-missing step.
